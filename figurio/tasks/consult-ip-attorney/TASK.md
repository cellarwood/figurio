# Consult IP Attorney

Engage a Czech/EU intellectual property attorney to review Figurio's business model and establish IP compliance guidelines.

## Scope

- Review the catalog sourcing model (commercial licenses, commissioned originals, revenue-sharing)
- Advise on AI-generated content IP implications (who owns an AI-generated 3D model?)
- Establish content moderation guidelines for the AI prompt system (which prompts must be rejected?)
- Review terms of service and customer agreements
- Advise on trademark registration for the Figurio brand
- Confirm compliance with EU e-commerce regulations (consumer rights, GDPR, distance selling)
