# Hire 3D Technician

Recruit a human 3D technician for manual QA review of AI-generated models before they enter the print queue.

## Details

- Role: part-time or freelance 3D technician
- Responsibilities: review AI-generated meshes for printability and aesthetic quality, perform manual mesh repairs that automated tools can't handle, approve models for printing
- Skills needed: 3D modeling experience (Blender, ZBrush, or similar), understanding of 3D printing constraints, attention to detail
- Platforms to source: ArtStation, Fiverr, Upwork, local Czech 3D printing communities
- Budget: to be determined based on expected volume of custom orders
- This is a human role, not an agent -- the ML Engineer's pipeline handles the automated portion
