# Create Brand Identity

Develop Figurio's complete brand identity: logo, color palette, typography, visual style, and tone of voice guidelines.

## Expected Subtasks

- CEO delegates to CMO
- CMO delegates visual execution to Content Creator
- Logo design (wordmark + icon, suitable for web, packaging, and social)
- Color palette (primary, secondary, accent colors)
- Typography (heading and body fonts for web and print)
- Visual style guide (photography style, illustration style, 3D render style)
- Tone of voice guidelines (how Figurio communicates across channels)
- Brand guidelines document consolidating all of the above
