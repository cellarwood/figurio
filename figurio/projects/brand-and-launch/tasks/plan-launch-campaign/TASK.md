# Plan Launch Campaign

Design the go-to-market campaign to drive awareness and first purchases at launch.

## Expected Subtasks

- CEO delegates to CMO
- Pre-launch: waitlist landing page, teaser social content, email capture
- Launch week: announcement posts, limited-edition launch figurine, discount code for first 50 orders
- Post-launch: user-generated content campaign (share your figurine, get a discount), referral program
- Budget allocation across channels (organic social, paid social, influencer seeding)
- Success metrics: waitlist signups, launch week orders, social followers, email subscribers
- Timeline: work backwards from storefront MVP launch date
