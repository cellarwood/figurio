# Brand and Launch

Establish Figurio's brand identity and execute the launch campaign to acquire the first 100 customers.

## Scope

- Brand identity (logo, color palette, typography, tone of voice)
- Social media presence (Instagram, TikTok)
- Launch campaign (pre-launch waitlist, launch announcement, first-week promotions)
- Product photography and content for the catalog
- Email marketing setup

## Deliverables

1. Brand guidelines document (logo, colors, typography, voice)
2. Social media accounts and initial content calendar
3. Launch campaign plan with timeline and budget
4. Product photography/renders for the initial catalog
5. Email marketing setup (welcome sequence, order confirmations)
