# Marketing

Owns brand identity, customer acquisition, and all marketing communications for Figurio. Drives awareness, engagement, and conversion through social media, content marketing, email campaigns, influencer partnerships, and seasonal promotions.

## Responsibilities

- Brand identity and guidelines (visual identity, tone of voice, positioning)
- Social media strategy and content (Instagram, TikTok, Facebook)
- Content marketing (blog, email newsletters, product descriptions)
- Campaign planning (seasonal, event-driven, product launches)
- Customer acquisition strategy (organic, paid, referral, influencer)
- Marketing analytics and KPI tracking (CAC, conversion rate, engagement)
- B2B marketing for corporate sales
