# Campaign Planning Framework

## Purpose

Standardize how Figurio plans and executes marketing campaigns. Every campaign — whether seasonal, product launch, or influencer-driven — follows this framework.

## Campaign Template

```markdown
# Campaign: {Name}

## Goal
Primary objective (e.g., 500 new customers, 20% revenue lift, 10K social impressions).

## Audience
Target segment(s) with demographics, interests, and buying motivation.

## Channels
Ranked by priority with budget allocation percentage.

## Content Mix
Types of content needed (posts, stories, blog, email, ads) with quantities.

## Timeline
Week-by-week execution schedule with milestones.

## Budget
Total spend with per-channel breakdown.

## Success Metrics
KPIs, targets, and measurement method for each.

## Post-Campaign Review Date
When and how results will be evaluated.
```

## Seasonal Campaign Calendar

### Q1: January - March

**Valentine's Day (Feb 14)**
- Theme: "Your Love in 3D" — custom couples figurines.
- Launch: Jan 15 (4-week lead for custom orders + MCAE print time).
- Content: Couple pose gallery, "design your pair" UX flow, gift guide.
- Channel focus: Instagram Reels (romantic content), Pinterest (gift ideas).
- Discount: 15% off couples figurines, free gift box packaging.

**Post-Holiday Recovery (Jan)**
- Retarget Q4 buyers with "New Year, New Collection" email campaign.
- Push gift card redemptions from holiday gifts.

### Q2: April - June

**Easter (variable date)**
- Theme: Easter-themed figurine collection (bunnies, spring motifs).
- Limited edition catalog items — scarcity drives urgency.

**Graduation Season (May-Jun)**
- Theme: "Graduate in 3D" — custom figurines in cap and gown.
- Target: Parents and friends of graduates.
- Channel focus: Facebook (parent demographic), Instagram.

**Wedding Season (May-Jun)**
- Theme: Custom wedding cake toppers.
- Partnership opportunity: wedding planning blogs and forums.

### Q3: July - September

**Gaming Convention Season (Jul-Sep)**
- Theme: Custom gaming character figurines.
- Tie-in with major conventions (GamesCom, PAX).
- Content: "Turn your game character into a real figurine."
- Channel focus: TikTok (gaming community), Reddit, Discord.

**Back-to-School (Aug-Sep)**
- Theme: Desk figurines, teacher appreciation gifts.
- Lower price tier focus (S/M sizes).

### Q4: October - December (Peak Season)

**Halloween (Oct 31)**
- Theme: Spooky figurine collection, custom monster designs.
- Launch: Oct 1 (tight window, rely on catalog items + fast customs).
- Content: "Figurines that go bump in the night" series.

**Black Friday / Cyber Monday (late Nov)**
- Theme: Biggest sale of the year.
- Offer: 25% off sitewide + free shipping on orders over threshold.
- Email sequence: teaser → early access → live → last chance.
- CRITICAL: Pre-stock popular catalog items with MCAE. Do not rely on print-on-demand during peak.

**Christmas (Dec 25)**
- Theme: "The Gift They'll Never Forget" — custom figurines as gifts.
- Launch: Nov 1 (order cutoff Dec 10 for guaranteed delivery).
- Content: Gift guides by recipient type, unboxing videos, family figurine sets.
- Channel focus: All channels, maximum spend.

## Influencer Campaign Structure

### Seeding Program

1. **Identify**: Find 20-50 micro-influencers (5K-50K followers) in niches: gaming, anime, tabletop, home decor, gift ideas.
2. **Seed**: Send free figurine (their choice from catalog or custom).
3. **Ask**: Unboxing video/post with honest review. No scripted content.
4. **Track**: Unique discount code per influencer (INFLUENCER15 pattern).
5. **Evaluate**: Cost per acquisition through each influencer's code.

### Partnership Tiers

| Tier       | Followers  | Compensation         | Content Expected        |
|------------|------------|----------------------|-------------------------|
| Nano       | 1K-5K      | Free figurine        | 1 post + 1 story        |
| Micro      | 5K-50K     | Free figurine + $50  | 1 post + 2 stories      |
| Mid-tier   | 50K-200K   | Free + $200-500      | 1 post + reel + stories |
| Macro      | 200K+      | Negotiate per deal   | Full content package     |

## Social Commerce Loop

The Figurio viral engine follows this cycle:

```
Figurine created → Customer receives → Photos/unboxing
    ↓                                        ↓
  Design more ←── Discover Figurio ←── Share on social
    ↓                                        ↓
  New order → Deliver → More photos → More shares
```

**Key enablers:**
- Include a branded photo card with every shipment: "Share your figurine #MyFigurio."
- Make the unboxing experience share-worthy (premium packaging, reveal moment).
- Feature user-generated content on Figurio's social channels (with permission).
- Offer a 10% referral discount for customers whose posts drive new orders.

## Channel Priorities

1. **Instagram** — Primary. Visual platform, figurine photography, Reels for process/unboxing.
2. **TikTok** — Viral potential. "Watch a figurine come to life" content, trending audio hooks.
3. **SEO / Blog** — Long-term. Target "custom 3D printed figurines" and related keywords.
4. **Email** — Retention. Post-purchase nurture, seasonal campaigns, reorder prompts.
5. **Pinterest** — Discovery. Gift idea pins, wedding toppers, home decor figurines.
6. **Facebook** — Targeted ads for older demographics (parents, gift buyers 35+).

## Campaign Review Checklist

After every campaign ends:
- [ ] Compare actual KPIs to targets.
- [ ] Calculate customer acquisition cost (CAC) per channel.
- [ ] Identify top-performing content and why it worked.
- [ ] Document lessons learned for next campaign.
- [ ] Update the seasonal calendar with refined dates and strategies.
