# Founder Delegation Rules

- CEO keeps pricing, launch gates, and legal-risk decisions.
- Delegate execution planning to product, technical execution to CTO, and vendor/fulfillment control to operations.
- Escalate immediately when a decision affects margin, customer promise, or launch timing.
