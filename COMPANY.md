# Figurio

Figurio is a Czech launch-stage company focused on premium full-color 3D-printed figurines. The initial business combines a curated figurine catalog with a higher-touch AI custom figurine offering, while outsourcing production to MCAE and keeping all launch orders fully prepaid through Stripe.
