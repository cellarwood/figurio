Conduct a weekly review of company progress.

## Agenda
1. Review progress against each company goal
2. Collect status updates from CTO, CMO, and Head of Operations
3. Identify blockers and assign resolution owners
4. Reprioritize tasks based on new information
5. Review budget and spending against plan
6. Update goal status and communicate changes

## Output
- Weekly status report shared with all agents
- Updated task priorities
- Blocker resolution assignments
