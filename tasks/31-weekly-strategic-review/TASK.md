Conduct weekly strategic review:

- Review progress against all 5 company goals
- Collect status updates from CTO, CMO, and Head of Operations
- Identify blockers and cross-team dependencies
- Reprioritize work if needed based on new information
- Track key metrics: development velocity, order pipeline readiness, marketing reach
- Document decisions and action items
- Communicate priorities for the coming week to all agents
