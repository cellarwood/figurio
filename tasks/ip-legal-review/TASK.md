Consult with a Czech/EU IP attorney to ensure Figurio's catalog and operations comply with intellectual property law.

## Topics to Cover

### Catalog IP Compliance
- Review model licensing terms for marketplace-sourced figurines (CGTrader, TurboSquid)
- Confirm that commercial licenses cover physical product resale
- Review work-for-hire contracts for commissioned original designs
- Review revenue-sharing agreements for designer partnerships

### AI-Generated Content (Phase 2 preparation)
- Legal status of AI-generated 3D models for commercial use in EU
- Liability for AI-generated models that inadvertently resemble copyrighted characters
- Content moderation requirements for AI prompt-based generation

### Customer-Facing Legal
- Terms of Service for the storefront
- Privacy Policy (GDPR compliance for EU customers)
- Refund and return policy for custom/printed products
- Cookie consent requirements

## Deliverables

- Attorney consultation completed
- Legal risk assessment document
- Draft Terms of Service
- Draft Privacy Policy
- Recommendations for content moderation policy (for Phase 2 AI generation)
