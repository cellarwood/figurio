name: Define pricing strategy
assignee: ceo
---

Set customer-facing prices for all figurine size tiers based on cost data and target margins.

## Inputs Needed

- MCAE per-unit printing costs (from Head of Operations)
- Shipping costs per destination zone (from Head of Operations)
- Packaging costs per tier (from Head of Operations)
- Stripe transaction fees (~2.9% + €0.25 per transaction)
- Platform overhead estimate (hosting, tooling, AI API costs)

## Pricing Decisions

- Set retail price per size tier: Small, Medium, Large
- Target: minimum 50% gross margin after MCAE + shipping + Stripe fees
- Consider psychological pricing (€19.90 vs €20.00)
- Define shipping pricing: flat rate, free above threshold, or actual cost
- Consider launch promotion: discount code or free shipping for first N orders

## Deliverables

- Pricing table: size tier × retail price × estimated margin
- Shipping pricing policy
- Launch promotion plan (if any)
- Decision documented and shared with CTO (for Stripe integration) and CMO (for marketing)
