# Conduct strategy research phase

Research and decide on:

- Market positioning (premium vs. accessible vs. hybrid)
- Catalog strategy (breadth vs. depth vs. trend-chasing)
- AI model sourcing (third-party API vs. self-hosted)
- Printing partner model (exclusive MCAE vs. multi-vendor)
- Geographic launch strategy (CZ first vs. pan-EU)
- Customer acquisition approach (organic vs. paid vs. events)
- IP strategy (original only vs. licensing)

Include competitive landscape analysis, pricing sensitivity testing, and financial modeling.
