Conduct structured strategy research to inform go-to-market decisions.

- Market positioning analysis: premium artisan brand vs. accessible mass-market vs. hybrid tiered approach
- Competitive landscape: identify direct competitors (other custom figurine services) and indirect competitors (Funko Pop, print-on-demand services)
- Pricing sensitivity: research willingness-to-pay across size tiers and custom vs. catalog
- Geographic strategy: Czech Republic first with EU expansion vs. pan-EU launch
- Customer acquisition: organic/social-first vs. paid acquisition vs. event/pop-up-first
- AI model sourcing: third-party API (faster launch) vs. self-hosted (better margins long-term)
- Document findings and recommendations for board review
