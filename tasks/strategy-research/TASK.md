Conduct the structured strategy research required before finalizing go-to-market.

- Market positioning: premium artisan brand vs. accessible mass-market vs. hybrid tiered approach
- Catalog strategy: breadth (many categories) vs. depth (few categories, many designs) vs. trend-chasing agility
- AI model sourcing: third-party API (faster launch) vs. self-hosted model (better margins long-term)
- Printing partner model: exclusive MCAE vs. multi-vendor supply chain
- Geographic launch: Czech Republic first with EU expansion vs. pan-EU from day one
- Customer acquisition: organic/social-first vs. paid acquisition vs. event/pop-up brand building
- Pricing sensitivity analysis: what price points maximize conversion across S/M/L tiers
- Competitive landscape: identify and analyze 5-10 competitors in the custom figurine / 3D print space
- Document findings and decisions in a strategy brief for the team
