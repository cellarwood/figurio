Research the competitive landscape for 3D-printed figurines.

## Research Areas
1. Direct competitors: other custom figurine services, pricing, quality, turnaround
2. Adjacent market: print-on-demand figurine services (HeroForge, Elecrow)
3. 3D printing technology trends: new printers, materials, color capabilities
4. AI-to-3D technology advances: new services, quality improvements, cost reductions
5. Pricing benchmarks across competitor size tiers
6. Customer review analysis on competitor products

## Output
- Competitive analysis document
- Pricing benchmark table
- Recommended strategic adjustments
