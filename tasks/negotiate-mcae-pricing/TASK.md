Establish production pricing with MCAE (mcae.cz) across all size tiers. This is a critical dependency — customer-facing prices cannot be set until per-unit production costs are locked in.

## Objectives

- Obtain per-unit pricing for Small (~8cm), Medium (~15cm), and Large (~25cm) figurines on the Stratasys J55 PolyJet
- Negotiate volume discounts (10+, 50+, 100+ units/month tiers)
- Agree on turnaround time SLAs (standard and rush)
- Define the print file submission workflow (format, delivery method, approval process)
- Explore drop-ship possibility (MCAE ships directly to customer)
- Discuss quality standards and reprint policy for defective units

## Coordination

- Head of Operations to lead the operational relationship after terms are agreed
- CTO to provide technical requirements for print file formats and model specifications

## Deliverables

- Signed pricing agreement or confirmed quote per size tier
- Volume discount schedule
- Turnaround time commitments
- Print file specification document
- Quality/reprint policy

## Success Criteria

- Per-unit costs allow minimum 50% gross margin at target retail prices
- Turnaround time supports 5-7 business day delivery for catalog items
- Clear process for submitting files and receiving finished products
