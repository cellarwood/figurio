Contact MCAE (mcae.cz) and negotiate per-unit printing pricing across all figurine size tiers.

## Objectives

- Get firm quotes for Small (~8cm), Medium (~15cm), and Large (~25cm) figurines
- Negotiate volume-based pricing tiers (1-10 units, 10-50, 50-100, 100+)
- Clarify turnaround times per tier and for different volumes
- Understand minimum order requirements (if any)
- Discuss quality standards and rejection/reprint policy
- Explore drop-shipping option (MCAE ships directly to customer)

## Deliverables

- Cost sheet with per-unit price by size tier and volume bracket
- Turnaround time estimates per tier
- Summary of SLA terms (quality guarantees, reprint policy, lead times)
- Recommendation to CEO on initial pricing based on target 50%+ gross margin

## Context

- MCAE uses Stratasys J55 PolyJet technology
- Figurines are full-color, display-grade quality
- This pricing data is critical for setting customer-facing prices — block other pricing decisions until complete
