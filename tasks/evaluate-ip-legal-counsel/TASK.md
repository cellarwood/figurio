Find and engage an IP attorney to advise on legal risks around AI-generated figurines.

- Research Czech/EU IP law firms with experience in e-commerce and intellectual property
- Key questions for legal counsel:
  - Liability for AI-generated models that resemble copyrighted characters (even unintentionally)
  - Content moderation requirements for the "Prompt to Print" feature
  - Terms of service and disclaimer language for AI-generated content
  - Commercial license requirements for models purchased from CGTrader/TurboSquid
  - GDPR compliance for customer data (accounts, orders, payment info)
  - EU consumer protection requirements (return policy, warranty, delivery guarantees)
- Get quotes from 2-3 firms
- Select counsel and schedule initial consultation
- Document legal requirements and incorporate into product and content guidelines
