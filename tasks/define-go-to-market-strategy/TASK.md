Lead the strategic research phase to determine Figurio's optimal go-to-market approach before committing resources to execution.

## Research Areas

1. **Market positioning** — premium artisan brand vs. accessible mass-market vs. hybrid tiered approach
2. **Catalog strategy** — breadth (many categories) vs. depth (few categories, many designs) vs. trend-chasing agility
3. **Geographic launch** — Czech Republic first with EU expansion vs. pan-EU from day one
4. **Customer acquisition** — organic/social-first vs. paid acquisition vs. event/pop-up brand building
5. **IP strategy** — fully original catalog only vs. licensing partnerships with select IP holders
6. **Pricing model** — cost-plus vs. value-based vs. competitive pricing across size tiers

## Deliverables

- Competitive landscape analysis (existing figurine/3D printing companies)
- Target customer persona definitions
- Pricing sensitivity analysis across size tiers
- Go-to-market recommendation document with rationale
- 90-day launch plan with milestones

## Coordination

- Work with CMO on customer acquisition strategy and brand positioning
- Work with Head of Operations on MCAE pricing to establish margins
- Work with CTO on AI pipeline timeline to sequence product launches

## Success Criteria

- Clear, documented strategy that the team can execute against
- Pricing validated against MCAE costs with healthy margins
- First 90 days of activities planned and assigned
