# Define Company Strategy

Conduct the strategy research phase outlined in the business plan to make data-driven go-to-market decisions.

## Key Decisions

- Market positioning: premium artisan brand vs. accessible mass-market vs. hybrid tiered
- Catalog strategy: breadth vs. depth vs. trend-chasing agility
- AI model sourcing: third-party API (faster) vs. self-hosted (cheaper long-term)
- Printing partner model: exclusive MCAE vs. multi-vendor supply chain
- Geographic launch: Czech Republic first vs. pan-EU from day one
- Customer acquisition: organic/social-first vs. paid vs. event/pop-up-first
- IP strategy: fully original catalog only vs. licensing partnerships

## Deliverables

- Competitive landscape analysis
- Pricing strategy with margin models per size tier
- Go-to-market plan with phased milestones
- Financial model (revenue projections, cost structure, break-even analysis)
