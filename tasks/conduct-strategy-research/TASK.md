Before finalizing go-to-market, conduct structured research across critical strategic dimensions.

## Research Areas

### 1. Market Positioning
- Analyze competitors: Heroforge, Shapeways, Etsy 3D-print sellers, local Czech figurine shops
- Determine positioning: premium artisan vs. accessible mass-market vs. hybrid tiered
- Identify defensible differentiators (AI-custom, PolyJet quality, Czech/EU-based)

### 2. Pricing Sensitivity
- Research comparable product pricing across competitors
- Model unit economics across size tiers once MCAE pricing is locked
- Define target margins per product line (catalog vs. AI-custom)
- Consider pricing tiers: standard, premium finish, rush processing

### 3. Geographic Launch Strategy
- Evaluate: Czech Republic first → EU expansion vs. pan-EU from day one
- Assess shipping cost and logistics complexity per region
- Identify highest-potential EU markets (Germany, UK, Netherlands, Nordics)

### 4. Customer Acquisition Strategy
- Evaluate: organic/social-first vs. paid acquisition vs. event/pop-up-first
- Estimate CAC targets per channel
- Identify initial customer segments to target (collectors, gift buyers, meme culture)

### 5. IP Strategy
- Confirm: fully original catalog only vs. licensing partnerships
- Consult Czech/EU IP attorney (identify and recommend firms)
- Define content moderation rules for AI-custom prompts

## Deliverables

- Strategy research document with findings, recommendations, and rationale per dimension
- Recommended go-to-market approach with timeline
- Risk assessment for chosen strategy

## Acceptance Criteria

- All 5 research areas covered with data-driven recommendations
- Clear recommendation for market positioning and pricing approach
- Geographic launch plan with Phase 1 market(s) identified
