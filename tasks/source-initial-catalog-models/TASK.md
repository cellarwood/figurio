Source and validate the first 20 3D figurine models for the Figurio catalog.

## Sourcing Channels

1. **Licensed marketplace models** (CGTrader, TurboSquid)
   - Must have verified commercial license for physical resale
   - Download and store license documentation
   - Budget: allocate per-model budget based on CEO approval

2. **Commissioned original designs**
   - Hire freelance 3D artists via ArtStation, Fiverr, or direct contracts
   - Full IP ownership by Figurio (work-for-hire agreement)
   - Brief: figurine-optimized, PolyJet-compatible, no IP infringement

3. **Community/designer partnerships**
   - Revenue-sharing arrangements with independent 3D artists
   - Figurio handles production, sales, fulfillment
   - Contract template needed

## Printability Validation (all models)

- Manifold (watertight) geometry — no holes or non-manifold edges
- Minimum wall thickness: 1mm for PolyJet
- No floating artifacts or disconnected geometry
- Support-structure compatibility check
- Test print at smallest tier to verify detail resolution

## Catalog Mix (target)

- 5 trending/meme-inspired designs
- 5 seasonal (Christmas, Halloween, general holiday)
- 5 evergreen collectibles (fantasy, sci-fi, animals)
- 5 gift-oriented (couples, family, desk decor)

## Deliverables

- 20 validated 3D models with license documentation
- Model metadata spreadsheet: name, category, source, license type, printability status
- Render previews for each model (for storefront product images)
