# Engineering Team

Responsible for building and maintaining the Figurio e-commerce platform, AI figurine pipeline, and supporting infrastructure.

## Scope

- React/TypeScript storefront (catalog, 3D viewer, checkout)
- FastAPI backend (product API, order management, Stripe integration)
- AI text-to-3D pipeline (prompt → generate → mesh repair → preview → print queue)
- Docker/K8s infrastructure and CI/CD pipeline
- Database design and migrations
