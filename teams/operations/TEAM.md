# Operations Team

Responsible for vendor management, order fulfillment, shipping logistics, quality assurance, and cost optimization.

## Scope

- MCAE printing partnership management
- Order fulfillment pipeline (order → print → QC → package → ship)
- Shipping logistics (Zasilkovna CZ, DHL EU/international)
- Quality assurance and defect tracking
- Cost-per-unit tracking and margin analysis
- Packaging design and branded unboxing experience
