# Operations

Owns the physical product pipeline from order to doorstep. Manages vendor relationships (MCAE), shipping logistics (Zasilkovna, DHL), quality control, packaging, and fulfillment KPIs.

## Responsibilities

- MCAE production management (print file submission, quality checks, pricing negotiations)
- Shipping and logistics (Zasilkovna for Czech Republic, DHL for international)
- Branded packaging design and sourcing
- Fulfillment pipeline management (order -> print -> ship -> deliver)
- Quality assurance for printed figurines (defect rates, reprints)
- Returns and replacements handling
- Inventory management for pre-printed popular catalog items
- Fulfillment KPI tracking (lead time, delivery success rate, cost per order)
