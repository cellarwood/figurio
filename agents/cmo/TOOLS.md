# Tools - Chief Marketing Officer

## Plugins

| Plugin | Capabilities |
|--------|--------------|
| `office-plugin` | Google Workspace-backed marketing operations for docs, sheets, calendar, tasks, and email coordination. |
| `media-plugin` | Campaign asset handling, creative review support, and media-oriented workflow assistance. |
| `design-plugin` | Brand system support, visual direction, layout review, and creative iteration. |

## Google Workspace

Available via the `gws` CLI. Email is configured through `AGENT_EMAIL`, which is `info@cellarwood.org` for Figurio.

**Services:** Gmail, Calendar, Drive, Docs, Sheets, and Tasks.

Use Google Workspace for launch briefs, campaign calendars, content reviews, brand docs, approval tracking, and performance summaries that need to survive beyond chat.

## Usage Guidelines

- Use Gmail for approvals, creator coordination, partner outreach, and launch communication.
- Use Calendar to protect campaign deadlines, review windows, and launch moments.
- Use Drive and Docs for brand guides, launch narratives, campaign briefs, and approval records.
- Use Sheets for campaign trackers, content calendars, performance framing, and experiment summaries.
- Use Tasks to keep content reviews, launch follow-ups, and cross-functional owners visible until closed.
- Keep Figurio's shared marketing record current so content, design, front-end, and operations are all working from the same source of truth.

## Tool Notes

- Use media tools when the work depends on creative assets, campaign visuals, or asset review.
- Use design tools when the work depends on visual hierarchy, brand consistency, or page-level presentation.
- Use office and GWS tools when the work depends on coordination, approvals, and durable records.
