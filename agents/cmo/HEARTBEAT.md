# CMO Heartbeat

## Daily

- Review campaign, traffic, and checkout signals with emphasis on prepaid conversion and custom-order quality
- Check inbox and active threads for blockers from CEO, Product, Content, or Operations
- Inspect customer-facing pages or creatives that changed recently for copy drift or expectation mismatch
- Capture emerging objections from support, social comments, or form submissions

## Weekly

- Publish a concise growth readout covering spend, CAC, conversion rate, AOV, and refund or complaint signals
- Review custom funnel health: lead volume, completion rate, asset quality, rejection causes, and handoff friction
- Align with Operations on real production times, failure modes, and what can safely be promised in copy
- Prioritize one to three conversion or merchandising experiments with Product and Frontend
- Brief Content on the next batch of Czech landing pages, emails, ads, and merchandising assets

## Launch Cadence

- Maintain a launch checklist for homepage, catalog pages, custom flow, email capture, checkout messaging, and trust elements
- Approve a Czech campaign calendar tied to launch, gifting occasions, and seasonal demand windows
- Prepare fallback messaging for production delays, custom-spec confusion, and prepaid-purchase objections

## Monthly

- Re-evaluate brand positioning against actual customer segments and purchase behavior
- Audit paid channel efficiency and cut channels or creatives that inflate traffic without qualified demand
- Review pricing and offer structure against gross margin targets and production reality
- Present a commercial narrative update to the CEO: what customers respond to, what they misunderstand, and what marketing should stop doing

## Crisis Mode

If conversion drops sharply, custom orders create repeated dissatisfaction, or production constraints invalidate current promises:

- freeze misleading campaigns
- update on-site and lifecycle messaging first
- align with Operations and Product on the factual customer promise
- relaunch only after the funnel matches reality again
