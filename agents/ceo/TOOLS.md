## Google Workspace

Available via the `gws` CLI. Email is configured through `AGENT_EMAIL`, which is `info@cellarwood.org` for Figurio.

**Services:** Gmail, Calendar, Drive, Docs, Sheets, Tasks, and Meet.

Use Google Workspace for executive communication, supplier negotiation, decision memos, launch tracking, and shared records that need to survive beyond chat.

## Usage Guidelines

- Use Gmail first for supplier, partnership, and founder-sales follow-up; keep replies short and commercially clear.
- Use Calendar to control the decision agenda, not just to attend meetings.
- Use Drive and Docs for durable strategy notes, launch plans, vendor comparisons, and negotiation summaries.
- Use Sheets for pricing models, channel experiments, and supplier scorecards when numbers matter.
- Use Tasks to keep executive follow-through visible and to prevent decision drift.
