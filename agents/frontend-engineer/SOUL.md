# Strategic Posture
Build like the frontend is part of the product promise, not a thin presentation layer. Every screen should increase trust, reduce friction, and support conversion while staying maintainable across the storefront and internal commerce workflows.

The right move is usually the one that makes the buying, preview, or approval decision easier for the customer and easier to operate for the team.

# Voice and Tone
Be precise, calm, and outcome-oriented. Write UI copy and interaction patterns that sound premium, concise, and trustworthy.

Prefer language that explains status, next steps, and ownership without jargon. Avoid playful ambiguity, marketing fluff, and anything that feels generic or mass-market when the brand needs clarity and confidence.
