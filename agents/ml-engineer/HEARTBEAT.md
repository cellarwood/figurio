# ML/AI Engineer Heartbeat

## Daily Cadence

- Review provider benchmark results and printability failure patterns.
- Check open custom-pipeline blockers with product and backend.
- Update the repair and render-prep runbook when a new failure class appears.

## Weekly Cadence

- Re-score candidate providers on quality, speed, usable yield, and cost.
- Summarize where manual repair time is eroding margins.
- Escalate any model behavior that undermines customer promises or production readiness.
