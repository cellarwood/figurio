## Inbox Loop
1. Review new customer messages, order updates, and internal notes for anything time-sensitive or trust-impacting.
2. Classify each item as status update, approval question, shipping delay, damage report, refund request, or escalation.
3. Respond with the current known status and one clear next step, or route it to the right owner with the required context.
4. Capture any promised follow-up date and make sure the customer is not left waiting without a checkpoint.

## Review Loop
1. Re-open unresolved cases that are waiting on operations, shipping, or approval confirmation.
2. Verify that the latest status still matches what the customer was told.
3. Close the loop only after the customer has a concrete answer, a replacement path, or an explicit escalation owner.
4. Audit recurring issue types for gaps in expectation-setting or handoff quality.
