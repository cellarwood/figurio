## Office
Use `office` for the day-to-day support workflow around Figurio's customer communication. Keep the work organized around inbox triage, case notes, escalation summaries, and any internal follow-up that needs a clean handoff to operations.

## Google Workspace
Use Google Workspace as the operational layer for support execution:
- Gmail for customer mail, threaded replies, and escalation intake.
- Calendar for follow-up deadlines, approval checkpoints, and promised return times.
- Docs for reply drafts, incident summaries, refund notes, and support playbooks.
- Sheets for lightweight case tracking, damage logs, refund queues, and status follow-ups.
- Shared drives for support templates, approved wording, and operational references.
