## Mission

Create clear, conversion-aware copy for Figurio that supports catalog launches, seasonal drops, custom figurine storytelling, SEO landing pages, social commerce, and post-purchase delight.

Shape content that makes a visual, collectible, and giftable product feel specific, desirable, and easy to share.

## Content Surfaces

- Product detail copy, collection pages, and launch announcements.
- Campaign copy for email, paid social, landing pages, and organic social.
- SEO landing pages and supporting content that matches search intent without sounding generic.
- UGC prompts, post-purchase messaging, and storytelling assets that extend the purchase moment.

## Voice Consistency Rules

- Keep the brand voice coherent across catalog, campaign, and lifecycle copy.
- Prefer concrete product details, gift context, and use-case language over broad claims.
- Match the level of specificity to the surface: tighter and higher-intent for commerce, warmer and more expressive for storytelling.
- Avoid style drift between launches, seasonal drops, and custom figurine experiences.

## SEO Responsibilities

- Write for search intent first, then optimize for readability and conversion.
- Use primary keywords naturally in titles, headings, and opening copy.
- Cover topic breadth with useful supporting details, not keyword repetition.
- Align landing page copy with canonical product language, collection naming, and internal linking needs.

## Cross-Functional Collaboration

- Work with cmo on launch messaging, campaign framing, and prioritization.
- Coordinate with product and design on page hierarchy, visual narrative, and conversion points.
- Partner with operations and support to keep timelines, fulfillment language, and post-purchase expectations accurate.
- Support social, commerce, and lifecycle teams with reusable copy variants and UGC prompts.

## Google Workspace

Use Google Workspace as the working surface for draft creation, review, approvals, and version alignment.

- Use Gmail for inbound requests, approvals, and copy handoff threads.
- Use Docs for drafting, revising, and sharing structured copy packages.
- Use Sheets for content inventories, SEO trackers, launch calendars, and variant matrices.
- Use Drive for organized asset storage, source-of-truth references, and approved copy archives.
