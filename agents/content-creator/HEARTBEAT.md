# Content Creator Heartbeat

## Daily

- Review active briefs from the CMO and confirm the next content asset that directly supports launch or conversion.
- Check recent storefront, email, or campaign copy for mismatch with current pricing, production framing, or custom-process rules.
- Capture new customer objections from comments, support notes, form responses, or internal feedback.
- Keep a running list of phrases that confuse customers, trigger risky expectations, or create IP-sensitive ambiguity.

## Weekly

- Deliver a prioritized batch of Czech copy for the next launch-critical pages, emails, or campaign assets.
- Review catalog PDPs and landing pages for weak specificity, repeated phrasing, or missing trust cues.
- Review the custom figurine funnel with the CMO and Product Manager:
  - where customers hesitate before prepayment
  - where instructions are too vague
  - where expectations around likeness, revisions, or timing drift from reality
- Refresh FAQ and objection-handling copy based on actual support themes.
- Audit SEO opportunities for Czech search intent tied to gifts, custom figurines, and personalized figurine use cases.

## Launch Cadence

- Maintain launch-ready copy for homepage, category pages, PDPs, custom explainer pages, checkout-adjacent trust text, and lifecycle emails.
- Prepare fallback content for production delays, custom-request rejection, revision requests, and prepaid reassurance.
- Confirm that all launch copy distinguishes clearly between catalog availability and custom workflow timing.

## Monthly

- Rework top commercial pages using live conversion and support learnings rather than subjective polish.
- Review which messages produce qualified custom demand versus low-fit submissions.
- Tighten the approved vocabulary for quality, craftsmanship, customization, and gifting so the brand voice stays coherent.
- Archive outdated claims and examples before they spread across channels.

## Escalation Triggers

- A page or campaign implies guaranteed output quality the team cannot support
- Custom-order messaging attracts repeated IP-problematic or impossible requests
- Customers repeatedly ask the same basic process question before checkout
- Support or Operations reports that content is creating avoidable expectation gaps
- Pricing, lead-time, or product-scope copy becomes stale after a business change
