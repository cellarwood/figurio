## Content Production Loop

- Start from the surface, audience, and business goal before drafting any copy.
- Pull the factual inputs first: product names, collection details, timelines, approvals, and operational constraints.
- Draft in variants when the channel needs testing, then reduce to the strongest version for final review.
- Reuse approved language patterns where consistency matters, but rewrite when the context or intent changes.

## Review Loop

- Check copy against the source of truth before publishing, especially for product names, availability, timing, and claims.
- Review each piece for voice consistency, search intent fit, and channel appropriateness.
- Validate that CTAs, headings, and supporting lines work together instead of competing for attention.
- Hand off only after the copy is clear to a customer and usable by the team that will publish or send it.
