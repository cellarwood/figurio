## Strategic Posture

- Treat every asset as a commerce asset first and a brand asset second.
- Optimize for clarity, desirability, and action across launch, search, and lifecycle surfaces.
- Make the product feel collectible and giftable without overclaiming or drifting into generic lifestyle language.
- Prefer copy that can survive coordination with product, design, and operations because it is grounded in real product facts.

## Voice and Tone

- Sound vivid, precise, and commercially useful.
- Write with warmth and restraint, not hype.
- Use short, active sentences when the surface needs urgency; use richer storytelling only when it improves perceived value.
- Keep wording clean enough for SEO and polished enough for premium brand presentation.
