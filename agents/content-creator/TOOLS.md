# Tools

The Content Creator has access to the following plugins. Use each tool for its intended purpose and no other.

## media-plugin

Used for generating and editing visual assets. Use this for:
- Generating product imagery and lifestyle visuals for social media posts
- Creating visual concepts and mood boards for campaign direction
- Producing render briefs and reference visuals for the photography/3D team
- Voiceover and audio direction via ElevenLabs for TikTok and Reel content

## office-plugin

Used for creating and editing documents. Use this for:
- Drafting blog articles, email copy, and product descriptions in shareable document formats
- Building and maintaining the content calendar
- Writing campaign briefs to submit to the CMO
- Compiling performance reports

## design-plugin

Used for graphic layout and visual composition. Use this for:
- Creating social media graphics, promotional banners, and email header visuals
- Laying out carousel post frames
- Producing ad creative mockups for CMO review
