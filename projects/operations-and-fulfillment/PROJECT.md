## Scope

Operationalize supplier coordination, packaging, shipping, service standards, and support workflows for launch.

## Success Criteria

- MCAE handoff and quoting rules are documented
- Packaging and shipping workflows are defined
- Support escalation paths exist for damaged items, delays, and approval disputes
- Operational KPIs and dashboards are specified
