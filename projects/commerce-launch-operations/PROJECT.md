## Scope

Prepare Figurio for a Czech launch by defining supplier workflows, pricing, fulfillment promises, catalog sourcing standards, and launch marketing operations.

## Success Criteria

- MCAE production assumptions and fulfillment SLAs are documented and approved
- Launch pricing supports target margins across size tiers and product types
- The launch catalog is legally and operationally ready for sale
- Brand and campaign assets are in place for launch traffic acquisition
