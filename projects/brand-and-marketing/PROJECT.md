## Scope

Establish Figurio's brand presence and drive initial customer acquisition.

## Key Deliverables

- Brand identity (logo, colors, typography, guidelines)
- Website copy (homepage, about, FAQ, product descriptions)
- Social media presence (Instagram, TikTok, X)
- SEO strategy and blog content calendar
- Launch campaign with pre-launch email capture

## Target Audience

- Collectors (fantasy, sci-fi, pop-culture figurines)
- Gift buyers (personalized, seasonal, occasion-based)
- Meme/internet culture enthusiasts
- Cosplayers and fandom communities

## Success Criteria

- Brand guidelines document complete
- Social accounts active with 10+ posts each
- SEO keywords ranking for target terms
- 100 paying customers within first quarter
