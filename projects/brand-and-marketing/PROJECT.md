## Scope

Establish Figurio's brand and drive initial customer acquisition.

### Key Deliverables
- Brand identity: logo, color palette, typography, tone of voice
- Product photography and catalog visuals
- Social media accounts (Instagram, TikTok, X) with content calendar
- SEO-optimized product descriptions and blog content
- Launch marketing campaign

### Brand Positioning
Figurio is positioned as a premium artisan brand — creative, playful, and accessible. Products are handcrafted-quality display pieces, not cheap trinkets.

## Success Criteria
- Consistent brand identity across all touchpoints
- Social media accounts active with regular posting
- Launch campaign driving measurable traffic
- Product pages ranking for target keywords
