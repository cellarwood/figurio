## Scope

Set up the complete production and fulfillment pipeline:

- Negotiate per-unit pricing with MCAE across all size tiers (Small, Medium, Large)
- Define the order-to-delivery SOP from payment to customer doorstep
- Specify branded packaging requirements and unboxing experience
- Integrate shipping providers (Zasilkovna for CZ, DHL/DPD for EU)

## Success Criteria

- Signed pricing agreement with MCAE covering all three size tiers
- Documented SOP for the full order-to-delivery workflow
- Branded packaging specs approved and sample produced
- Shipping integration tested with real parcels (Zasilkovna + DHL)
- Per-order cost model documented with margins calculated
