## Scope

Build the launch storefront for Czech customers with catalog browsing, AI custom request intake, Stripe checkout, order confirmation, and order status visibility.

## Success Criteria

- Customers can browse catalog products and start a purchase without manual intervention
- The custom figurine request flow captures all information needed for the downstream pipeline
- Stripe payments are captured successfully for catalog and custom orders
- The team has a basic order tracking view for customer communication and operations
