## Scope

Establish the Figurio brand and drive initial customer acquisition. Includes brand identity design (logo, colors, typography), social media channel setup (Instagram, TikTok), product photography pipeline using 3D renders, and the first seasonal marketing campaign.

## Success Criteria

- Consistent brand identity across all touchpoints
- Active social media presence with regular posting cadence
- Product photography pipeline producing catalog-ready images
- First campaign launched with measurable conversion tracking
