# Marketing Launch

Brand building and content marketing project for Figurio's market entry. Creates the brand identity, launches social media presence, and produces initial content to build awareness and drive traffic to the storefront.

## Scope

- Brand identity design (logo, colors, typography, visual language)
- Social media channel setup (Instagram, TikTok, Twitter)
- Initial content calendar (4 weeks)
- Product photography direction and visual asset library
- SEO foundation for the storefront
- Email marketing templates (welcome series, order confirmation)

## Success Criteria

- Complete brand kit (logo, color palette, typography, photography style)
- Active social profiles on Instagram, TikTok, and Twitter
- 500 followers across platforms within first 60 days
- 4-week content calendar published and executing
- SEO-optimized product descriptions for all catalog items
