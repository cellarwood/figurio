Build a 4-week content calendar for social media and blog launch.

- Plan 3+ social media posts per week per platform (Instagram, TikTok, Twitter)
- Mix content types: product showcases, behind-the-scenes, how-it's-made, customer stories
- Identify seasonal and trending opportunities for the first month
- Plan 2 blog posts for launch month (e.g., "How PolyJet 3D Printing Works", "Gift Guide: Custom Figurines")
- Schedule email welcome series (3 emails: welcome, product showcase, first purchase incentive)
- Document calendar in Google Sheets with dates, platforms, content type, and status
