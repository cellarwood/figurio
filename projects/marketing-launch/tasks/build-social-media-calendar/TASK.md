Create and populate a 4-week social media content calendar for Figurio's launch on Instagram and TikTok.

## Content Calendar Structure

### Weekly cadence (per platform)
- **Instagram:** 4 posts/week (2 product showcases, 1 behind-the-scenes, 1 community/engagement)
- **TikTok:** 3 videos/week (product reveals, AI generation process, unboxing concepts)
- **Stories/Reels:** daily or every other day

### Content Themes for Launch Month

**Week 1: "Meet Figurio"**
- Brand introduction posts
- "What is Figurio?" explainer
- First product reveals

**Week 2: "The Magic of Prompt to Print"**
- AI generation process showcase
- Before/after: prompt → figurine
- "What would you create?" engagement post

**Week 3: "Made for Gifting"**
- Gift occasion suggestions
- Size comparison and pricing hints
- Customer use case stories (hypothetical for launch)

**Week 4: "Coming Soon / Launch Countdown"**
- Countdown posts
- Early access or waitlist CTA
- Launch day announcement

## Deliverables

- Content calendar spreadsheet (date, platform, content type, caption, visual direction, CTA)
- Draft captions for all 4 weeks of posts
- Visual direction notes for each post (what image/video to create)
- Hashtag strategy per platform

## Acceptance Criteria

- 4-week calendar completed with all posts planned
- Captions written and reviewed by CMO
- Visual direction clear enough for image/video production
- No IP-infringing content in any post
