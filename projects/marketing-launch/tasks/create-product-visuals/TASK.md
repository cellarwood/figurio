Produce visual content for the Figurio catalog and marketing channels.

## Deliverables

### Product Visuals
- Product render concepts for 6-8 catalog figurines (using AI image generation or 3D renders)
- Each figurine shown in: clean white background, lifestyle setting (desk, shelf, gift box), and detail close-up
- Size comparison images showing Small/Medium/Large tiers

### Lifestyle Photography Direction
- Mood board: figurines in real-world contexts (desks, shelves, gift-giving moments, unboxing)
- Seasonal concepts: Christmas figurines with holiday decor, Valentine's couples figurines
- "Prompt to Print" process visualization: prompt text → 3D preview → physical figurine

### Social Media Assets
- Instagram grid template (consistent visual style across posts)
- TikTok thumbnail concepts
- Story/Reel frame templates

## Technical Requirements

- Images at least 1200x1200px for social media
- Product images at 2000x2000px for web (with WebP optimization)
- Consistent color treatment aligned with brand style guide

## Acceptance Criteria

- 6-8 product visual sets completed
- Lifestyle mood board shared with team
- Social media template concepts delivered to CMO for review
