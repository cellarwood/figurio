Write 3 blog posts for the Figurio launch, targeting SEO keywords and building brand awareness.

## Blog Post 1: "About Figurio"
- **Target keyword:** custom 3D printed figurines
- **Length:** 600-800 words
- **Content:** Company story, what makes Figurio different (PolyJet quality, curated catalog, original designs), the vision
- **CTA:** Browse the catalog

## Blog Post 2: "How We Make Your Figurines"
- **Target keyword:** 3D printed figurines how it works
- **Length:** 800-1000 words
- **Content:** Behind-the-scenes of the process — design sourcing, printability validation, Stratasys J55 PolyJet technology, quality control, packaging
- **CTA:** Order your first figurine
- **Note:** Do not mention MCAE by name — present it as "our production partner" or "our printing facility"

## Blog Post 3: "The Ultimate Gift Guide: 3D Figurines for Every Occasion"
- **Target keyword:** unique gift ideas figurines
- **Length:** 800-1000 words
- **Content:** Gift ideas organized by occasion (birthday, Christmas, Valentine's, housewarming), by recipient type (gamer, fantasy fan, animal lover), by budget (S/M/L tiers)
- **CTA:** Shop the collection, with links to specific products

## Technical Requirements

- Each post: H1 title, H2/H3 subheadings, meta description (<160 chars), 2-3 internal links to products
- Markdown format, ready for CMS import
- Images: suggest product shots to accompany each section
