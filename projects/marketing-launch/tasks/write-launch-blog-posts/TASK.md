Write the first two blog posts for the Figurio website, optimized for SEO and designed to explain the product and build excitement.

## Blog Post 1: "How AI Creates Your Custom Figurine"

- **Target keywords:** custom 3D figurine, AI figurine maker, personalized figurine, text to 3D print
- **Length:** 1000-1500 words
- **Structure:**
  1. Hook: "Imagine describing anything and holding it in your hands"
  2. How it works: step-by-step (prompt → AI generation → preview → print → delivery)
  3. What makes it special: PolyJet technology, full color, detail quality
  4. Examples of prompts and results
  5. CTA: try it yourself / join waitlist

## Blog Post 2: "Meet Figurio — Your New Favorite Way to Gift"

- **Target keywords:** unique gifts, personalized gifts, 3D printed gifts, custom figurines
- **Length:** 800-1200 words
- **Structure:**
  1. Hook: the problem with generic gifts
  2. Figurio's solution: catalog + custom
  3. Gift occasions: birthdays, holidays, weddings, desk decor
  4. Size options and what to expect
  5. CTA: browse the catalog / create your own

## SEO Requirements

- Meta title (under 60 chars) and meta description (under 160 chars) for each post
- Internal linking structure (link between posts and to product/catalog pages)
- Header hierarchy (H1 → H2 → H3)
- Image alt text suggestions

## Acceptance Criteria

- Both posts written, SEO-optimized, and submitted to CMO for review
- Meta titles and descriptions included
- No IP-infringing content or unverifiable claims
- Tone matching brand voice guidelines
