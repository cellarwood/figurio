Define Figurio's brand identity and visual language.

- Brand story: the "why" behind Figurio (craftsmanship meets AI innovation, personal and giftable)
- Logo usage guidelines (placement, spacing, minimum sizes, backgrounds)
- Color palette: primary, secondary, and accent colors with hex codes
- Typography: heading and body fonts for web and marketing materials
- Photography style: how to photograph figurines (lighting, backgrounds, angles, lifestyle shots)
- Illustration style: graphic elements, patterns, and decorative motifs
- Tone of voice summary: friendly, enthusiastic, visual, approachable
- Social media visual templates (Instagram post, story, TikTok thumbnail)
- Email header and footer templates
- Compile into a brand guidelines document (PDF or Markdown)
