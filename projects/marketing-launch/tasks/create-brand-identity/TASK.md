Define the Figurio brand identity that will be used across the storefront, packaging, and all marketing materials.

## Deliverables

### Brand Guidelines Document
- **Logo concepts:** Direction for the Figurio wordmark and icon (to be executed by a designer or AI tools)
- **Color palette:** Primary, secondary, and accent colors with hex codes. Should convey: premium quality, creativity, fun
- **Typography:** Font selections for headings and body text (web-safe or Google Fonts)
- **Visual style:** Photography direction (clean backgrounds, lifestyle shots, macro detail shots), illustration style
- **Tone of voice:** Writing style guide for all customer-facing copy

### Packaging Direction
- Unboxing experience concept: what the customer sees when they open the box
- Box sizing for S/M/L figurine tiers
- Branded elements: logo placement, color scheme, thank-you card insert
- Material suggestions (eco-friendly preferred)

## Context

- Figurio positions as premium but accessible — not luxury, not cheap
- Target audience: 25-45, gift-givers, collectors, pop-culture enthusiasts, desk decor buyers
- Competitor reference points: Funko Pop (mainstream), Sideshow Collectibles (premium), Redbubble (custom/quirky)
