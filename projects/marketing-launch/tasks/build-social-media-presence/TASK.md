Set up Figurio's social media presence on Instagram and TikTok and create the initial content.

## Requirements

### Account Setup
- Instagram business account: bio, profile image, link to figurio.cellarwood.org
- TikTok account: bio, profile image, link to storefront

### Initial Content (10+ posts per platform)

**Instagram:**
- Product showcase posts (single figurine, clean background, multiple angles)
- Lifestyle shots (figurine on a desk, bookshelf, as a gift)
- Behind-the-scenes: 3D printing process, packaging
- Size comparison posts (figurine next to everyday objects)
- Collection/grid posts (multiple figurines arranged together)

**TikTok:**
- Unboxing videos (short, satisfying reveal)
- "Making of" clips (3D model to printed figurine)
- Size reveal videos (expectations vs reality, but positive)
- Gift reaction concepts
- Trending audio/format adaptations featuring figurines

### Content Calendar
- 4-week posting schedule: 3-4 posts/week per platform
- Mix of content types across the calendar
- Hashtag strategy per platform

## Style Notes

- Instagram aesthetic: clean, bright, consistent color grading
- TikTok style: casual, authentic, slightly humorous
- Always include CTA: link in bio, product tag, or "shop now" prompt
