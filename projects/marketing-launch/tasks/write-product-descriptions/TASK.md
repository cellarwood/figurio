Write SEO-optimized product descriptions for the first 20 catalog figurines.

## Requirements per Product

- **Title:** descriptive, keyword-rich (e.g., "Steampunk Dragon Desktop Figurine")
- **Short description:** 1-2 sentences for catalog grid cards
- **Full description:** 150-250 words for product detail page
  - Lead with emotional appeal (how it looks, feels, what it evokes)
  - Include size tiers and approximate dimensions
  - Mention material: full-color PolyJet resin, smooth finish, display-grade quality
  - Suggest use cases: desk decor, gift, collectible
- **SEO meta description:** under 160 characters
- **Tags/categories:** for catalog filtering

## Style Guide

- Warm, approachable, slightly playful tone
- Sensory language: describe textures, colors, how the figurine looks on a shelf
- No IP-infringing references — describe archetypes, not specific copyrighted characters
- Include a subtle CTA: "Add to your collection" or "Makes a perfect gift"

## Delivery

- Markdown file per product, or a single document with all 20 organized by category
- Ready for import into the product database
