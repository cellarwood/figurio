## Scope

Establish the Figurio brand and launch marketing channels.

### Key deliverables

- Visual brand identity (logo, color palette, typography, imagery style)
- Product photography pipeline and initial asset library
- Social media presence (Instagram, TikTok) with content calendar
- SEO-optimized blog with launch content (5-10 articles)
- Email marketing setup for product launches and promotions
- Brand guidelines document for consistent messaging

## Success Criteria

- Brand guidelines approved and documented
- Social media accounts active with initial content
- Blog live with SEO-optimized content
- Email list capture functional on the storefront
