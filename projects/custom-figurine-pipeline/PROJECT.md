## Scope

Design and implement the custom figurine workflow from prompt intake through preview approval and release to production.

## Success Criteria

- Customers can submit prompts and enter a deposit-backed flow
- Generation jobs and review states are tracked reliably
- Approval and revision handling is defined and testable
- Release to production requires both approval and payment completion
