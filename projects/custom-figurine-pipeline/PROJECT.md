## Scope

Build the internal workflow for text-to-3D generation, mesh repair, rendering, printability review, moderation, and approval handoff.

## Success Criteria

- The team can generate, repair, and review custom figurines against a repeatable process
- Unprintable or policy-violating custom requests are detected before production handoff
- Internal tooling captures review status, approval decisions, and final production assets
