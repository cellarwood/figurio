Research and plan Phase 2 scan-to-print service:

- Scanning equipment evaluation:
  - Photogrammetry rig (30-60 camera array): cost, setup time, portability, output quality
  - Structured-light scanner (Artec Leo): cost, ease of use, scan quality
  - Software: mesh processing, texture mapping, figurine scaling
- Pop-up event targeting: comic cons, Christmas markets, corporate events in Czech Republic
- Event logistics: booth requirements, power, setup/teardown time, staffing
- Pricing model: per-scan price, bundle discounts, event-specific promotions
- Demand validation: target 50 scans across first 3 events
- Cost model: equipment amortization, per-scan costs, break-even analysis
