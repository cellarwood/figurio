## Scope

Establish the end-to-end production and fulfillment pipeline for Figurio's figurine business.

## Key Deliverables

- MCAE pricing negotiation and partnership agreement
- Shipping provider setup (Zasilkovna, DHL)
- Branded packaging design for S/M/L size tiers
- Fulfillment SOP documentation
- IP/legal review with Czech/EU attorney

## Key Partners

- MCAE (mcae.cz) — Stratasys J55 PolyJet printing
- Zasilkovna — Czech/EU parcel delivery
- DHL — International shipping

## Success Criteria

- Per-unit pricing finalized for all size tiers
- Shipping accounts active with rate cards
- Fulfillment SOP documented and tested
- First test order shipped successfully
