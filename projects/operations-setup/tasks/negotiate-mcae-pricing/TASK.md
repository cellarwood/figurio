# Negotiate MCAE Pricing

Establish per-unit printing costs with MCAE (mcae.cz) for all three size tiers to determine product margins and customer-facing pricing.

## Expected Subtasks

- CEO delegates to Head of Operations
- Contact MCAE and request quotes for Stratasys J55 PolyJet printing
- Get pricing for: Small (~8cm), Medium (~15cm), Large (~25cm) figurines
- Negotiate volume tiers (1-10, 11-50, 51-100+, 100+ units/month)
- Clarify turnaround times per volume tier
- Explore drop-ship option (MCAE ships directly to customer)
- Document all pricing in a spreadsheet for CEO review
- Determine minimum viable margins for each size tier
