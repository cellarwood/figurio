Establish the MCAE printing partnership.

## Requirements
- Contact MCAE (mcae.cz) and establish Figurio as a business client
- Negotiate per-unit pricing for each size tier:
  - Small (~8cm): target under 500 CZK
  - Medium (~15cm): target under 1,200 CZK
  - Large (~25cm): target under 2,500 CZK
- Define turnaround SLAs (target: 3-5 business days for catalog, 5-7 for custom)
- Negotiate volume discounts at 50, 100, 500 unit thresholds
- Explore drop-ship option (MCAE ships directly to customer)
- Establish communication channel and order submission process
