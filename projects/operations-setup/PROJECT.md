## Scope

Establish Figurio's production and fulfillment operations.

### Key Systems
- MCAE partnership with negotiated pricing per size tier
- Order-to-print workflow: file prep → submission → QA → packaging → shipping
- Shipping integration: Zásilkovna (Czech Republic), DHL (EU)
- Branded packaging and unboxing experience
- Stripe payment reconciliation with production costs

### Production Partner
- MCAE (mcae.cz) — Stratasys authorized partner
- Stratasys J55 PolyJet printer
- Full-color, multi-material prints

## Success Criteria
- Per-unit pricing negotiated across all size tiers
- Fulfillment SOP documented and tested
- Shipping integration working for CZ and EU
- End-to-end order turnaround under 10 business days for catalog items
