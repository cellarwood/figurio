## Scope

Build Figurio's brand presence and drive initial customer acquisition.

### Core Workstreams

- Brand identity: logo, color palette, typography, visual guidelines
- Website content: landing page copy, product descriptions, FAQ, about page
- SEO: keyword research (3D printed figurines, custom figurines, personalized gifts), blog plan, metadata
- Social media: Instagram and TikTok accounts, content calendar, influencer outreach
- Launch campaign: promotions, referral program, PR outreach
- Product visuals: 3D renders, lifestyle photography, social media assets

### Key Channels

- Instagram (visual product showcase)
- TikTok (custom figurine creation process, unboxings)
- Blog/SEO (long-tail organic traffic)
- Influencer partnerships (3D printing, collectibles, gift guides)

## Success Criteria

- Brand identity complete and applied across all touchpoints
- 20+ product descriptions and 10+ blog posts published
- Social accounts active with content calendar in place
- Launch campaign plan ready with influencer pipeline
- Product renders available for all catalog items
